## User Cards variants

The UserCards component has 4 variants: full, simple, large and contact

And full and vertical have 2 different layouts: horizontal and vertical

Full variant:

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group position={'apart'}>
    <UserCards user={stories.user} />
    <UserCards user={stories.user} layout={'vertical'} />
  </Group>
</ThemeProvider>

Simple variant:

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group position={'apart'}>
    <UserCards user={stories.user} variant={'simple'} />
    <UserCards user={stories.user} variant={'simple'} layout={'vertical'} />
  </Group>
</ThemeProvider>

Large variant and contact variant (with error):

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group position={'apart'}>
    <UserCards user={stories.user} variant={'large'} />
    <UserCards user={stories.user} variant={'contact'} error={'Contact already added'} />
  </Group>
</ThemeProvider>

## User card states

And full, simple and large variant has a selected state

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group>
    <UserCards user={stories.user} variant={'large'} selected />
    <UserCards user={stories.user} selected />
    <UserCards user={stories.user} variant={'simple'} selected />
