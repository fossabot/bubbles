## Checkbox variants

The checkbox component has two variants: default and boxed.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Checkbox label="Checkbox 1"></Checkbox>
  <Space></Space>
  <Checkbox label="Checkbox 2" variant="boxed"></Checkbox>
</ThemeProvider>

## Help positions

You can also place the help text to the right or bottom of the checkbox label.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Checkbox label="Default checkbox 1" help="Right help"></Checkbox>
  <Space></Space>
  <Checkbox label="Default checkbox 2" help="Bottom help" helpPosition="bottom"></Checkbox>
  <Space></Space>
  <Checkbox variant="boxed" label="Boxed checkbox 1" help="Right help"></Checkbox>
  <Space></Space>
  <Checkbox
    variant="boxed"
    label="Boxed checkbox 2"
    help="Bottom help"
    helpPosition="bottom"
  ></Checkbox>
</ThemeProvider>
